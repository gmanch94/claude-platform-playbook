As of 2026-06. Pin to current surface — refresh monthly.

Read the centre first, then the branches. The whole change is the middle box: you stop doing the task and start directing and checking it. Each branch is one reflex to shift — old habit on top, new habit in green. The small amber line is the trap if you swing too far; ignore it until you've made the basic shift.

How to use it. Hand it to someone in week one next to the cheatsheet — the map is the glance, the cheatsheet is the detail. The two things that have to land even at a glance are in the middle (you own what ships) and the bottom (the data boundary). Everything green is the direction to move.
