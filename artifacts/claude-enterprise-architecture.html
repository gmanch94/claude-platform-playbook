Claude Platform Playbook

Claude Enterprise Architecture As of 2026-07

The full Claude platform feature surface an Enterprise organization can deploy — access & identity, human + developer surfaces, capabilities, models, deployment paths, and the governance spine that runs through all of them. A feature-surface map, not a data-flow diagram. Every box is grounded in feature-inventory.md (the source of truth); status tags (GA / beta / preview) are current as of the stamp. Verify surface + governance at platform.claude.com before committing.
Read it top-down. Users and agents enter through Access & Identity; they act through surfaces; surfaces are powered by capabilities; capabilities run on models; models are consumed through a deployment path. The Governance & Compliance spine at the base is not a layer you pass through once — it is cross-cutting, and on Enterprise it is the differentiator. Beta / preview surfaces carry weaker governance guarantees (see the caveat at the bottom).

Governance is per-surface — do not inherit coverage. The BAA is not automatic: an Enterprise admin must activate HIPAA and sign, and the covered-feature list differs between the first-party API and the product surface — verify it at signing. Cowork and Claude Design are BAA-excluded; Claude Tag and Claude Science have BAA/ZDR/residency unstated — treat as not-covered until confirmed. ZDR does not cover the Enterprise product UI. Fable 5 is not ZDR-eligible. Bedrock/Vertex/Azure use the hyperscaler's own residency, not inference_geo. Full per-feature detail: enterprise-data-boundaries.html · governance-overlay.html · procurement-pack.html. Not legal advice.
